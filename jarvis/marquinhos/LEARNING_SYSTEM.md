# LEARNING_SYSTEM.md — Continuous Learning Framework

*How Marquinhos learns, persists patterns, and compounds over time.*

---

## Learning Stores

### `/learning/creative/`
- Approved creatives and their performance metrics
- Rejected creatives and rejection reasons
- Winning hooks and what made them work
- High CTR visual styles and patterns
- Platform-specific creative patterns

### `/learning/conversion/`
- Conversion patterns and what drives them
- Landing page performance data
- CTA effectiveness by audience segment
- Funnel step optimization data

### `/learning/campaigns/`
- Campaign-specific learnings
- Before/after comparisons
- Budget efficiency data
- Timeline performance data

### `/learning/brand/`
- Brand guide references (colors, typography, logo rules)
- Brand compliance patterns
- Visual identity consistency data

## Autonomous Detection Rules

Marquinhos continuously monitors for:

### Performance Signals
- **Weak CTR pages** — pages with CTR below benchmark
- **Stale creatives** — assets older than 90 days with declining performance
- **Missing thumbnails** — blog posts or listings without thumbnails
- **Outdated blog visuals** — blog posts with default/placeholder images
- **Weak marketplace listings** — listings with low CTR or conversion rate
- **Poor-performing product images** — products with low click-through rates

### Opportunity Signals
- **Seasonal opportunities** — upcoming holidays, events, or trends
- **Competitor creative shifts** — competitors launching new creatives or campaigns
- **Content gaps** — topics with high search volume but low competition

---

## Autonomous Response Workflow

When a signal is detected:

1. **Log the signal** in the appropriate `/learning/` directory
2. **Analyze the impact** — what's affected and how much
3. **Suggest a response**:
   - For weak CTR: propose creative refresh
   - For stale creatives: propose redesign
   - For missing thumbnails: propose image generation
   - For seasonal opportunities: propose new campaign
   - For competitor shifts: propose competitive analysis
   - For content gaps: propose content plan
4. **Prepare a brief** for human approval
5. **Escalate to user** with recommendation

---

## Compound Improvement Loop

```
Execute → Measure → Learn → Optimize → Execute
```

1. Execute creative/content assets
2. Measure performance against KPIs
3. Learn what worked and what didn't
4. Optimize next iteration based on learnings
5. Execute improved version
6. Repeat
